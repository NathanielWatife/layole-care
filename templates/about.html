who we are?

Operating around the clock, Layole hospital is home to a team of exceptionally skilled doctors, registered nurses, laboratory scientists, and other dedicated team members committed to ensuring optimal patient care.
Nestled at the prestigioourus address mentioned, our state-of-the-art facilities include:

  • Luxurious patient waiting lounges
  • Elegant consulting rooms
  • Well-appointed private wards
  • Comfortable general wards
  • Thoughtfully designed male and female wards
  • Efficient nurse stations
  • Sophisticated dispensary unit
  • A 24-hour standby power supply with controlled air conditioning ensures reliable backup power.

In addition to our comprehensive medical services, we feature distinguished consultants specializing in various fields:

  • Obstetrics and Gynaecology
  • Consultants
  • General Surgeons
  • Paediatric care
  • Internal Medicine
  • Orthopedic Surgeons

Layole and Sons Hospital Limited stands as a prestigious private healthcare institution, meticulously registered under the Corporate Affairs Commission and accredited by the Health Facility Monitoring and Accreditation Agency (HF). Our journey commenced on May 1, 2008, and since inception, we have embodied a steadfast dedication to excellence in delivering top-tier healthcare services.

Furthermore, we proudly offer physiotherapy services as part of our comprehensive restorative and rehabilitative care systems. At Layole and Sons Hospital Limited, our unwavering commitment is to provide an environment conducive to healing and healthcare excellence. Your well-being takes precedence, and we eagerly anticipate the opportunity to serve you with the utmost professionalism and expertise.
