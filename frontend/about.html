Our Story

Layole and Sons Hospital Limited stands as a prestigious private healthcare institution, meticulously registered under the Corporate Affairs Commission and accredited by the Health Facility Monitoring and Accreditation Agency (HF). Our journey commenced on May 1, 2008, and since inception, we have embodied a steadfast dedication to excellence in delivering top-tier healthcare services. Furthermore, we proudly offer physiotherapy services as part of our comprehensive restorative and rehabilitative care systems. At Layole and Sons Hospital Limited, our unwavering commitment is to provide an environment conducive to healing and healthcare excellence. Your well-being takes precedence, and we eagerly anticipate the opportunity to serve you with the utmost professionalism and expertise.
